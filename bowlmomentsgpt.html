The student problem

Why Bowl Moments exists

College food shouldn’t drain your energy or your wallet. At Presidency University, we saw the same pattern every day: skipped breakfasts, late-night Maggi, and random oily snacks between classes. Fast, yes. Nourishing, not really.

What’s going wrong

Low energy in lectures and labs, hair fall and skin issues, and money spent on food that fills you up but doesn’t actually nourish you.

Our simple fix

Balanced vegetarian bowls that are affordable enough to eat often, easy to pre-order, and built to quietly cover your everyday nutrition.

Our story

Two students, one health wake-up call

We’re two students from Presidency University, Bangalore who hit the same health wall: low energy, frequent fatigue, and scary levels of hair fall. After blood tests and doctor visits, we kept hearing the same thing — “You need better nutrition, not just supplements.”

That’s when we realised most of our meals came from canteen counters or quick delivery apps. Fast, tasty, but low on vegetables, protein, and actual nutrients. Bowl Moments started as a way to fix our own plates and slowly became a way to help other students do the same.

Our mission
  • Make healthy eating genuinely easy for busy students.
  • Keep bowls student-priced, not “premium health food”.
  • Cut the junk and focus on clean, vegetarian ingredients.
  • Build a student-to-student food brand you can actually trust.
